▐ Welcome to #ACERS!

#Acers: Connecting Car Enthusiasts

Welcome to #Acers, where we bring car enthusiasts together through our unique app prototype. Catering to those who share a passion for cars, driving, and socializing, our app provides a platform to connect with like-minded individuals within the community. However, the real thrill lies in the ability to collect crates and lootboxes to enhance and customize your beloved cars.


Revolutionizing Virtual Racing: A Unique Approach

Car lovers crave the thrill of racing, but we understand the ethical concerns associated with it. In response, we've introduced a virtual racing feature. Users can virtually "race" each other, and the app determines the winner based on the technical specifications of the cars involved. Whether it's drifting, drag racing, or other categories, #Acers ensures a fair and exciting competition.


Project Briefing:

For this project, my two friends and I poured our passion into creating #Acers. Our goal was to effectively communicate information to a specific audience, be it through a website, web app, or application tailored to the needs of our target users. The emphasis was on language and presentation that resonates with the community. Importantly, we adhered to ethical standards, ensuring that our project aligns with legality and morality. Join us in exploring the world of #Acers where the love for cars and ethical engagement converge.

▐ Persona: Yuki

The Ultimate Car Enthusiast

Meet Yuki, the embodiment of passion for cars and the thrill of driving. Yuki finds joy not just in cruising down the road but in sharing his enthusiasm with others and keeping track of every detail. For him, life is a constant competition, but until now, there's been no single platform that fully caters to his diverse needs.


Addressing Yuki's Needs: Crafting the Ultimate Car Enthusiast App

Our mission is to develop an app that not only meets but exceeds Yuki's expectations, making his car enthusiast journey challenging, social, and deeply personal.


1. Challenging: Virtual Racing and Comparison

- Introducing a virtual racing feature, where Yuki can compete against other car enthusiasts in various categories like drifting and drag racing.

- Enabling quick car spec comparisons, providing the thrill of competition without the need to search through endless details.


2. Social: Community Interaction and Events

- Creating a vibrant social platform where Yuki can share his passion, experiences, and insights with fellow car lovers.

- Facilitating connections by organizing events and meetings where enthusiasts can come together, share stories, and delve into their mutual love for cars.


3. Personal: In-App Upgrades and Customization

- Offering in-app upgrades that allow Yuki to personalize his car, from changing its color to upgrading its rims or even exploring different car models.

- Tailoring the app experience to Yuki's preferences, making every interaction and upgrade a reflection of his unique style.


In essence, #Acers aims to be the ultimate destination for car enthusiasts like Yuki, providing a challenging, social, and deeply personal experience that transcends the traditional boundaries of car-related platforms. Let's embark on this journey together, where the road is limitless, and the passion for cars knows no bounds.

▐ Log-in Process

How it works

Explore the initial steps of the #Acers app as users navigate the log-in process. The sequence begins with a straightforward zero state, prompting either a log-in or sign-up. Returning users can seamlessly choose their car and enter the app.


Newcomers

During sign-up, have the option to input their car's license plate. The system recognizes and associates their vehicle, confirming its accuracy. Users can adjust this information as needed. The app ID now extends beyond just an identifier, incorporating the license plate for a personalized touch. This integration marks the commencement of a unique #Acers journey.

▐ Main / Home Screen

Where it all begins

Welcome to the heart of #Acers, the Home Screen, where your automotive adventure unfolds. Following a successful login, your car takes center stage, visually captivating and ready to hit the virtual road. Below, a sleek navigation bar offers three tabs for further exploration.

▐ The Licenscan Feature

What is licenscan?

Unveiling a distinctive feature within #Acers – the Licenscan, a creative solution to provide users with the thrill of racing without compromising ethical standards. The idea is to instill a sense of competition, allowing users to feel a competitive edge without engaging in real-world racing risks.


How It Works:

Accessible from the home screen, the Licenscan feature offers a unique experience. Users can input someone's license plate or capture an image for the app to scan – hence the name Licenscan. Once you've scanned another car, the app recognizes and retrieves information about the vehicle. Discover details like top speed, drift race potential, drag race capabilities, and street race prowess.


Compare-Race Functionality:

Take it a step further with the Compare-Race function. After scanning a car, engage in a virtual race comparison. The app calculates and presents the technical outcome of a race, determining who would emerge victorious in a drift race, drag race, or street race.


The Licenscan feature adds a competitive yet ethical dimension to the #Acers experience, letting users virtually test their racing mettle against others.

▐ Final Reflection and Closure

As I revisit this project, it's been a journey through the early days of my career as a CMD student. A time of exploration and learning, particularly in the realms of UI, design, and layout. The project, centered around #Acers, brought a unique challenge and an opportunity to immerse myself in the world of automotive enthusiasts.


Project Development:

The collaborative efforts, especially fueled by the briefing, allowed us to infuse rich content into the app. The result is, in my opinion, an aesthetically appealing and intriguing interface. Working on a project within my own demographic was not only fun but also an insightful experience, requiring extensive research and effort.


Challenges and Learnings:

Reflecting on the project, I recognize the need for better planning, especially in mapping out the necessary screens and functionalities. Balancing simplicity with a feature-rich application posed some challenges. I found it particularly challenging to navigate ethical considerations, especially in devising an alternative to real racing. The introduction of the Linenscan feature proved to be a creative and ethically sound solution. This experience has deepened my understanding of ethical design practices.


Team Collaboration:

The collaborative effort with my team has been instrumental. While my focus leaned more towards design, the team's collective research efforts significantly contributed to the project's depth.


Looking Forward:

In the future, I aim to refine my planning process, ensuring a smoother navigation between features and functionalities. The experience with #Acers has left me content and eager to explore further. A shoutout to my team for their valuable contributions, and perhaps, there's room for future expansions and enhancements to this exciting automotive app!
